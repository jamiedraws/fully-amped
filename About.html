About Us

Fully Amped stands out as the only officially licensed CrossFit Affiliate Apparel provider, a distinction we've proudly held for five years. During our time in the industry, we've formed partnerships with over 3000 CrossFit gyms, demonstrating our deep understanding and commitment to the CrossFit community. Our dedication to quality is evident in our choice of garments, sourced exclusively from renowned blanks manufacturers such as Next Level Apparel, Bella + Canvas, and Independent Trading Company.

Our recent acquisition by Fabritech has further enhanced our capabilities. Based in Michigan, Fabritech is a family-owned business with a rich history dating back to 1993. They've produced over 50 million garments, blending the art of garment-making with advanced technology. Fabritech's dedication to excellence and its role as a trusted merchandise partner for numerous large-scale corporations set it apart in the industry.

The synergy between Fully Amped's specialized CrossFit knowledge and Fabritech's technical expertise and state-of-the-art equipment offers unparalleled value. This partnership empowers your business to soar to new heights while we expertly manage your merchandise needs, ensuring quality, innovation, and a focus on sustainability.
